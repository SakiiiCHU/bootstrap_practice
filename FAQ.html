Frequently Asked Questions

This platform provides a space for artists to showcase their artwork digitally, enabling them to reach a global audience while reducing the costs of physical exhibitions.
Artists can register by visiting the 'Artist Registration' page, where they will be asked to fill in their details, upload their artwork, and set their pricing. Once approved, their profile will be featured on the platform.
Tickets can be purchased from the 'Exhibitions' section. Simply choose the exhibition you wish to attend, select the ticket type, and complete your purchase via our secure payment system.
You can purchase artwork directly from the artist's profile. Once you select a piece of art, follow the instructions for checkout and payment to complete the purchase securely.
To manage your profile settings, log in to your account, visit the 'Account Settings' page, and update your personal information, password, or payment details.
